## In-App Support

### Pro & Trial Users

If you're a Pro subscriber or currently in your free trial, you can submit feedback directly from the app:

1. Open MedMemoir app
2. Go to **Profile** tab
3. Tap **Settings**
4. Under "Help & Support", tap **Send Feedback**
5. Choose **Bug Report** or **Feature Request**
6. Describe your issue or suggestion

---

## Response Time

We strive to respond to all support inquiries within 24-48 hours during business days. For urgent issues affecting app functionality, we'll prioritize your request.

---

## What to Include in Your Support Email

To help us assist you more quickly, please include:

- **Your email address** associated with your MedMemoir account
- **Device information:** iPhone/Android model and OS version
- **App version:** Found in Profile → Settings → About
- **Description of the issue:** What happened and what you expected to happen
- **Steps to reproduce:** How can we recreate the issue?
- **Screenshots:** If applicable, screenshots help us understand the problem

**Important:** Never include sensitive medical information, passwords, or payment card details in your support emails. Our team will never ask for your password.

## Emergency Medical Situations

**MedMemoir is not for medical emergencies.** If you're experiencing a medical emergency, please call emergency services (911 in the US) or visit your nearest emergency room immediately.

MedMemoir is a records management tool and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.
