# Privacy Policy for MedMemoir

**Last Updated: November 24, 2024**

**Krocess LLC** ("we," "us," or "our") operates the MedMemoir mobile application (the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Service.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Personal Information
We collect information that you provide directly to us:
- **Account Information**: Email address, password (encrypted)
- **Profile Information**: Name, date of birth, blood type, sex, phone number, address, profile photo
- **Medical Information**: Health records, vital signs, medical documents, allergies, conditions, medications, disease history
- **Emergency Contacts**: Names and contact information of your emergency contacts

### 1.2 Health Data
MedMemoir is a medical records management application. We collect and store:
- Vital signs (blood pressure, heart rate, glucose levels, weight, temperature, etc.)
- Medical documents and photos
- Medication information and reminders
- Appointment information
- Health data synced from Apple Health or Google Health Connect (with your permission)

### 1.3 Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
- **Usage Information**: App features used, timestamps, interaction data
- **Technical Data**: IP address, app version, crash reports

### 1.4 Camera and Photo Library
- Camera access for measuring heart rate and HRV using photoplethysmography
- Photo library access for saving and managing medical documents and profile photos

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Provide, maintain, and improve the Service
- Store and manage your medical records securely
- Send email verification codes and notifications
- Process your subscription payments
- Respond to your support requests and feedback
- Comply with legal obligations
- Detect and prevent fraud or security issues

## 3. Data Storage and Security

### 3.1 Encryption
- All data is encrypted at rest using industry-standard encryption (AES-256)
- Data is stored locally on your device using secure storage (iOS Keychain/Android Keystore)
- Cloud backups (if enabled) are encrypted

### 3.2 HIPAA Compliance
We implement reasonable and appropriate safeguards designed to protect your health information, consistent with HIPAA requirements for mobile health applications.

### 3.3 Data Retention
- Your data is stored on your device and optionally in Firebase Cloud Storage
- You can delete your data at any time from within the app
- We retain minimal server logs for 90 days for security purposes

## 4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

### 4.1 We DO NOT Sell Your Data
We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal or health information.

### 4.2 Service Providers
We may share information with third-party service providers who assist us:
- **Firebase (Google)**: Cloud storage and authentication
- **Apple/Google**: In-app purchases and health data sync
- **Email Service**: For verification emails and support (Resend or similar)

These providers are contractually required to protect your data and use it only for providing services to us.

### 4.3 Legal Requirements
We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government request.

### 4.4 Emergency Situations
In medical emergencies, emergency contacts you've added can access your medical profile via QR code (only if you've enabled this feature).

## 5. Your Rights and Choices

### 5.1 Access and Control
You have the right to:
- Access all your data within the app
- Edit or delete your information at any time
- Export your medical records
- Delete your account and all associated data

### 5.2 Communication Preferences
You can control:
- Email notifications
- Medication and appointment reminders
- Push notifications

### 5.3 Health Data Permissions
You can revoke access to Apple Health or Google Health Connect at any time through your device settings.

## 6. Children's Privacy

MedMemoir is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you are a parent/guardian and believe your child has provided us with information, please contact us.

## 7. International Users

MedMemoir is operated from the United States. If you are located outside the U.S., your information will be transferred to and processed in the United States. By using the Service, you consent to this transfer.

## 8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by:
- Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
- Sending an email notification (if you've provided an email address)
- Updating the "Last Updated" date

Your continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

## 9. California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):
- Right to know what personal information is collected
- Right to know if personal information is sold or disclosed
- Right to say no to the sale of personal information (we don't sell data)
- Right to access your personal information
- Right to delete your personal information
- Right to equal service and price

## 10. Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach that affects your personal or health information, we will notify you within 72 hours via email and in-app notification.

## 11. Third-Party Services

### 11.1 Apple Health and Google Health Connect
If you choose to sync data with Apple Health or Google Health Connect:
- Data is shared according to permissions you grant
- We only read data you explicitly authorize
- You can revoke permissions at any time in your device settings

### 11.2 Payment Processing
Payment information is processed by Apple App Store or Google Play Store. We do not store credit card information.
